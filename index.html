Who We Are

Empowering Citizens with Digital Access

CSC Manwan is an authorised Village Level Entrepreneur (VLE) centre operating under the CSC e-Governance India Limited framework — a flagship initiative of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Government of India.

We bridge the digital divide by providing over 100 government and private sector services at a single point, making it easy and accessible for every citizen in our community.
