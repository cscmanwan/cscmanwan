Who We Are

Empowering Citizens of Manwan

CSC Manwan is an officially authorised Village Level Entrepreneur (VLE) centre under CSC e-Governance Services India Limited — a flagship initiative of the Ministry of Electronics & IT, Government of India.

We serve the citizens of Manwan and surrounding areas in Jammu & Kashmir by providing over 100 government, banking, health, agriculture, and private services at a single accessible point — eliminating the need to travel to distant cities for routine government work.
